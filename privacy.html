Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data N8n Automation ("the App") accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored when you connect your TikTok account.

1. Data We Access

DataSourcePurpose
TikTok OAuth access token & refresh tokenTikTok Login KitTo authenticate API requests and publish video content on your behalf
TikTok user ID / open IDTikTok Login KitTo identify which account a video should be posted to
Upload status (e.g. processing, published, failed)TikTok Content Posting APITo confirm whether a scheduled video was successfully published

2. Data We Do Not Access

The App does not request access to your TikTok direct messages, contacts, browsing history, or any personal data beyond what is listed above. The App does not read or collect data from other users' accounts.

3. How Data Is Stored

Access and refresh tokens are stored only within the operator's private, self-hosted automation environment (n8n). They are never shared with, sold to, or made accessible to any third party. Tokens are used solely to make authenticated requests to TikTok's official API endpoints.

4. Data Retention

Tokens are retained only for as long as the App remains connected to your TikTok account. If you revoke access via TikTok's "Manage Third-Party Apps" settings, the App's ability to use the token is immediately terminated, and stored tokens are deleted from the automation environment on the next scheduled cleanup.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services as part of generating video content, none of which receive your TikTok account data:

  • An AI text-generation service, to write video scripts from a topic list;
  • An AI image-generation service, to create video scene images;
  • A text-to-speech service, to generate narration audio.

Only the final rendered video file (image, audio, and captions already combined) is sent to TikTok for publishing.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's account settings. You may also request deletion of any stored token data by contacting the operator directly.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by the account operator only and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
