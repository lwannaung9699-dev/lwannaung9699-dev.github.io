🪐 Space Story Auto Poster

An automation tool that generates short-form space & astronomy videos and publishes them to TikTok.

What this is

Space Story Auto Poster is a personal automation workflow (built with n8n) that runs on a fixed schedule to create and publish short educational videos about space and astronomy topics — black holes, nebulae, planets, and more — to a single, explicitly-authorized TikTok account.

How it works

  1. Selects a space/astronomy topic
  2. Generates a short narrated script using an AI text model
  3. Generates matching scene images using an AI image model
  4. Synthesizes voice narration with text-to-speech
  5. Assembles everything into one vertical video with captions and background music
  6. Publishes the finished video to the authorized TikTok account via TikTok's official Content Posting API

Who uses this

This tool is used solely by its developer for their own TikTok content. It does not serve, host, or act on behalf of any other user or business.
