Automated space & astronomy videos, published straight to TikTok

A personal automation tool that writes, illustrates, narrates, and publishes short-form educational videos about space — on a fixed schedule, to a single authorized TikTok account.

What this app does

📝 Script generation

An AI language model writes a short narrated script for each space topic, structured into a multi-part series.

🎨 Scene illustration

An AI image model generates a matching cinematic visual for every scene in the script.

🎙️ Voice narration

Text-to-speech turns each scene's script into natural spoken narration.

🎬 Video assembly

Scenes are combined into one vertical video with captions, background music, and smooth transitions.

📤 Publishing

The finished video is uploaded directly to the authorized TikTok account via TikTok's official Content Posting API.

👤 Single-user only

This tool serves only its own developer's TikTok account. It does not act on behalf of any other user.
